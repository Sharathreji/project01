About Us

PEACE FOUNDATION (Pachomios Educational And Charitable Enlightenment Foundation) was established to perpetuate the sacred memory of His Grace Joseph Mar Pachomios on 24th July 1997. Under the patronage of St. George Orthodox Syrian Church, Karmelkunnu, Mulakulam (N), this foundation prioritizes health care with its multi-specialty hospital in Piravom.

Our Mission

To provide state-of-the-art medical attention at affordable expenses, catering to the healthcare needs of the inhabitants of Piravom and surrounding areas.
