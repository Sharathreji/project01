PEACE FOUNDATION (Pachomios Educational And Charitable Enlightenment Foundation) was established to perpetuate the sacred memory of His Grace Joseph Mar Pachomios on 24th July 1997 under the patronage of St. George Orthodox Syrian Church, Karmelkunnu, Mulakulam (N). Mar Pachomios is the true symbol of Christian virtues. He dedicated his whole life to the cause of the poor and the needy, the down-trodden and the ill-treated. The members of the foundation decided to give primary importance to health care and go ahead with the ambitious task of establishing a Multi-specialty hospital at Piravom.

Our Mission

The concept of a Multi-specialty hospital is a boon to the inhabitants of this area, satisfying the needs of thousands of people in and around Piravom. The hospital aims to provide state-of-the-art medical attention at affordable expenses to the common man.
